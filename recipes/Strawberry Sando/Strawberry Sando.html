Strawberry Sando

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of milk bread
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 4-5 fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • Optional: additional strawberries for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream with sugar and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.
  2. Spread a generous layer of whipped cream on one slice of milk bread.
  3. Arrange the sliced strawberries on top of the whipped cream.
  4. Place the second slice of milk bread on top to create a sandwich.
  5. Gently press down and cut the sandwich into halves or quarters.
  6. Optionally, garnish with additional strawberries. Serve immediately or refrigerate for a short time before serving.
